How it Works

Keystroke dynamics is a behavioral biometric that measures the unique rhythm of your typing. Dwell Time measures how long a key is pressed down, and Flight Time measures the interval between releasing one key and pressing the next. By analyzing these micro-timings, neural networks can establish a typing signature to authenticate users continuously and transparently without password friction.
